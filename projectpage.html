Project Title: Rain Shield Auto Hanger


Detail explanation about our project given bellow:-

A Rain Shield Auto Hanger project is typically a mechanical-electrical system designed to automatically protect clothes from rain when they are hung outside to dry. This is especially useful in places where people donâ€™t have covered balconies or laundry areas and depend on sunlight for drying clothes. The system detects rain and pulls the clothes inside or covers them with a protective shield automatically, preventing them from getting wet.

Objective: To design and develop an automatic system that detects rainfall and either retracts the hanger indoors or covers the clothes using a mechanical rain shield to prevent clothes from getting wet.

Working Principle: The system uses a rain sensor to detect moisture. When rain is detected, a microcontroller (like Arduino) triggers a motorized mechanism (DC/Stepper motor or linear actuator) to either: Retract the clothesline into a protected area (e.g., balcony ceiling or wall cabinet), or Deploy a waterproof shield (e.g., plastic or acrylic cover) over the clothes. Once the rain stops, the system can either wait for user input or automatically return to the original position.

Major Components: 1. Rain Sensor Detects rainfall through moisture detection or water conductivity. Types: YL-83 or FC-37 rain sensor module. Sends a signal (LOW) to microcontroller when rain is detected.

2. Microcontroller (e.g., Arduino UNO, ESP32) Acts as the brain of the system. Takes input from the rain sensor and sends output signals to control the motor.

3. Motor Used to retract the clothesline or move the shield. Types: DC Motor (for rolling mechanism) Stepper Motor (for precision movement) Linear Actuator (for vertical shield movement)

4. Motor Driver Module (e.g., L298N) Provides sufficient power and control signals to the motor from the microcontroller.

5. Clothes Hanger or Rail The structure that holds clothes. Can be designed as: Retractable rail Rolling hanger Fixed rail with a movable rain cover

6. Rain Shield (if covering mechanism used)

A transparent or waterproof plastic/acrylic sheet mounted on a sliding frame. 7. Power Supply

Battery or AC-DC adapter (usually 5V/12V depending on motor type). 8. Limit Switches / IR Sensors (optional)

To detect end positions (fully open or fully closed) to stop the motor automatically.

Features:

Automatic detection of rain

Hands-free operation

Protection of clothes from sudden rain

Low power consumption

Can be integrated with mobile apps (IoT upgrade)

Applications:

Residential apartments

Hostels / Dormitories

Laundry services

Hospitals and hotels

Rural areas without covered drying space

Advantages:

Prevents clothes from getting wet unexpectedly

Reduces user effort

Can be customized for different spaces

Cost-effective automation project

Limitations:

Initial setup cost

Requires proper calibration

Power dependency (unless solar-powered)

Future Enhancements:

Integrate with IoT (mobile app alerts, remote control)

Solar-powered version

Auto-redeployment after rain stops

Wind sensor integration to avoid damage in storms

Foldable / collapsible structure for space saving

Conclusion:

The Rain Shield Auto Hanger is a practical and innovative solution to a common household problem. It combines basic sensors, automation, and mechanical design to ensure clothes remain dry even in unpredictable weather. It's a perfect example of how simple technologies can greatly enhance everyday convenience.
